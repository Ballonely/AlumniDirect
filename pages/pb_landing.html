About the University

A Century of
Carolinian Excellence

The University of San Carlos, rooted in Cebu since 1595, stands as one of the oldest institutions of learning in the Philippines. Entrusted to the Society of the Divine Word (SVD) since 1935, USC has grown into a premier Catholic research university committed to the formation of competent, socially responsible, and morally upright individuals.

From its historic Main Campus in Cebu City to its specialized campuses, USC upholds a tradition of academic rigor across the natural sciences, engineering, business, law, education, and the arts, producing graduates who lead in every field and every corner of the world.
