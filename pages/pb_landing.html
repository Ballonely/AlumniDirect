About the University

A Century of
Carolinian Excellence

The University of San Carlos, rooted in Cebu since 1595, stands as one of the oldest institutions of learning in the Philippines. Entrusted to the Society of the Divine Word (SVD) since 1935, USC has grown into a premier Catholic research university committed to the formation of competent, socially responsible, and morally upright individuals.

From its historic Main Campus in Cebu City to its specialized campuses, USC upholds a tradition of academic rigor across the natural sciences, engineering, business, law, education, and the arts, producing graduates who lead in every field and every corner of the world.

Our Graduates

The Carolinian Alumni

Spanning generations and industries, USC alumni carry the Carolinian mark, a commitment to faith, excellence, and service wherever life takes them, a testament to the University's education with a mission.

Academic Distinction

USC alumni are trained across rigorous programs spanning engineering, medicine, law, business, and the liberal arts, equipped with both technical mastery and critical thought that defines a Carolinian.

Global Reach

From Cebu to every continent, USC graduates lead organizations, pioneer research, and serve communities worldwide. The Carolinian network is a living testament to the university's reach and influence beyond its campus walls.

Lifelong Community

Dugtong Carolinian exists to keep Carolinians connected, to each other, to the university, and to the shared values instilled in every graduate. This directory is your bridge back to that community.
